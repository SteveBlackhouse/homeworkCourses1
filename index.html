Project
Easy parking

Easy Parking (February 2018 – now) - is designed to help drivers quickly find a free space, improve parking space and ease traffic. Sensors embedded in the ground will determine whether parking spaces are occupied or free. Drivers will be able to get real-time parking information. Drivers will be able to easily find a free space using a map when only a few parking spaces are available.

Technologies: C/C++, microcontrollers

Role:Hardware developer(working with ESP32, that works with Lora)
